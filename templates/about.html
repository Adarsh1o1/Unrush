How It Works

Welcome to Unrush! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make the most of our file hosting platform.

1. Sign Up and Get Started

  • Create an Account: Signing up is simple and free. Just click the “Sign Up” button, fill in your details, and you're ready to go!
  • Log In: If you already have an account, log in with your credentials to access your files.

2. Upload Your Files

  • Easy Upload: Navigate to your dashboard and click on the "Upload" button. You can upload files of various types and sizes directly from your device.
  • Organize in Folders: Create folders to keep your files organized. Group similar files together for quick access.

3. Manage Your Files and Folders

  • File Management: Rename, delete, or move files between folders with just a few clicks.
  • Folder Organization: Use folders to categorize and structure your files for easy retrieval.

4. Share Files with Others

  • Link Sharing: Want to share a file or folder with others? Generate a unique link for your file or folder and share it with anyone.
  • Permissions Control: Decide who can view, download, or edit the files you share to ensure your data remains secure.

5. Access Files Anywhere

  • Cross-Device Compatibility: Access your files from any device. Simply log in to your account from a smartphone, tablet, or computer, and your files will be there.
  • Automatic Sync: Any changes you make will automatically sync across your devices, so you always have the latest version.

6. Enhanced Security

  • Data Encryption: We use encryption to protect your data, ensuring that only you and the people you share with have access.
  • Privacy Control: Your files are all yours. They cannot be manipulated(creation, updation and deletion) by others.

7. Download Anytime

  • Quick Downloads: Download your files whenever you need them. Just click the "Download" button, and the file will be saved to your device.
  • Bulk Download: Download multiple files or entire folders at once for convenience.

Start Now!

Ready to make the most of Unrush? Log in to your account and explore the world of seamless file management and sharing. Your files, always at your fingertips.
